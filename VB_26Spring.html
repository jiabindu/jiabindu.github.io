    Syllabus (planned topics)



    Part 1: Positivity of vector bundles

    1.1 Basics on vector bundles
    1.2 Harder-Narasimhan filtrations
    1.3 Positivity of vector bundles
    1.4 Ample vector bundles on elliptic curves
    1.5 Geometric applications

    Part 2: Moduli spaces of vector bundles on curves

    2.1 Construction of moduli spaces
    2.2 Cohomology groups
    2.3 Chow groups

    Part 3: Syzygy problems

    3.1 N_p properties
    3.2 Fujita Conjecture

    References:

    Algebraic Surfaces and Holomorphic Vector Bundles, Robert Friedman
    The Geometry of Moduli Spaces of Sheaves, Daniel Huybrechts and Manfred Lehn
    Lectures on Vector Bundles, J.Le Potier
    Positivity in Algebraic Geometry, Robert Lazarsfeld
