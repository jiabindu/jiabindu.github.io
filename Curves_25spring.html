    Syllabus

    Week 1-5

    Goal: Basics on algebraic varieties (介绍代数簇的基本概念):


    1. Projective algebraic varieties(射影代数簇)
    2. Morphisms and rational maps (态射和有理映射)
    3. Irreducibility and dimension (不可约性和维数)
    4. Algebraic curves and smooth varieties(代数曲线和光滑簇)
    5. Resolution of singularities (奇异曲线的奇点消解)
    6. Intersection theory and Bezout Theorem (射影空间中的相交理论及Bezout Theorem )

    Week 6-10

    Goal: Riemann-Roch Theorem and applications(介绍复曲线的 Riemann-Roch 定理):


    1. Divisors and vector bundles (除子和向量丛)
    2. Cohomology (上同调)
    3. Serre duality and Riemann-Roch Theorem
    4. Riemann-Hurwitz formula
    5. Special curves: rational,elliptic,hyperelliptic

    Week 11-15

    Goal: Geometry of projective curves(介绍复射影曲线的几何)：


    1. Embeddings into projective space and canonical maps (到射影空间的嵌入、典范映射)
    2. Embeddings into abelian variety and Albanese morphisms (到阿贝尔簇的嵌入、Albanese态射)
    3. Clifford inequality
    4. Castelnuovo inequality
    5. Brill-Noether theory (Brill-Noether理论)

    References:

    1. Algebraic Geometry , Robin Hartshorne; Springer; 1977
    2. Geometry of algebraic curves I, Arbarello-Cornalba-Griffiths-Harris;1985
    3. Algebraic Geometry I: algebraic curves, algebraic manifolds and schemes ,I.R. Shafarevich (Ed.); 1994
    4. Algebraic Curves , W. Fulton; 2008
