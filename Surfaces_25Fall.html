    Email: jiabin.du@simis.cn

    Course information:

    Course time: every Tuesday 9:55-12:30 a.m., at SIMIS 1610

    Pre-requisite Courses

    Commutative Algebra, Algebraic Geometry

    Syllabus

    Week 1-5

    Chapter 1: Basics on algebraic surfaces


    • 1.1 Cubic surfaces. Note 1, Sep 9
    • 1.2 Curves on surfaces and intersection numbers. Note 2, Sep 16
    • 1.3 Blow-ups, rational maps and linear systems. Note 3, Sep 23
    • 1.4 Structure of birational morphisms and Castelnuovo contractibility theorem. Sep 28
    • 1.5 ADE, rational and cyclic singularities. Sep 30

    Week 6-12

    Chapter 2: Classification theory


    • Nakai-Moishezon Criterion, Hodge index theorem and Kodaira dimension, Oct 14
    • Ruled surfaces and rational surfaces, Oct 21
    • Elliptic surfaces and canonical bundle formula
    • K3 surfaces and Enriques surfaces
    • Abelian surfaces and Albanese varieties
    • Bi-elliptic surfaces
    • Enriques-Kodaira classification via MMP

    Week 13-14

    Chapter 3: Fibrations of algebraic surfaces


    • Slope inequality
    • Hyperelliptic fibrations
    • Canonical fibrations
    • Albanese fibrations
    • Fibrations of low genus

    Week 15-16

    Chapter 4: Geography of surfaces of general type


    • Miyaoka-Yau inequality
    • Noether, Debarre and Severi inequalities
    • Geometry of pluri-canonical maps

    References:

    • Chapters on Algebraic Surfaces, M. Reid
    • Complex Algebraic Surfaces, A. Beauville
    • Compact Complex Surfaces, BHPV
    • Classification of Complex Algebraic Surfaces, C. Ciliberto
    • The fibrations of algebraic surfaces, G. Xiao
    • Surfaces fibrées en courbes de genre deux, G. Xiao
    • Works of A. Beauville, F. Catanese, O. Debarre, E. Horikawa, G. Xiao, X. Lv-K. Zuo, M. Barja, R. Pardini, L. Stoppino and etc
