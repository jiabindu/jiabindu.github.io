    Email: jiabin.du@simis.cn

    Office: SIMIS 1619

    Course information:

    Course time: every Tuesday 9:55-12:30 a.m., at SIMIS 1610

    Pre-requisite Courses

    Commutative Algebra, Algebraic Geometry

    Syllabus

    Week 1-6

    Goal: Basics on algebraic surfaces


    1. Cubic surfaces, rational scrolls
    2. Curves on surfaces and intersection numbers
    3. Surfaces and singularities

    Week 7-10

    Goal: Classification theory


    1. Positivity of divisors
    2. Enriques-Kodaira classification on surfaces

    Week 11-13

    Goal: Fibrations of algebraic surfaces


    1. Slope inequality
    2. Hyperelliptic fibrations
    3. Canonical fibrations
    4. Albanese fibrations
    5. Fibrations of low genus

    Week 14-16

    Goal: Geography of surfaces of general type


    1. Miyaoka-Yau inequality
    2. Noether and Debarre inequalities
    3. Castelnuovo inequality
    4. Severi inequality

    References:

    1. Complex Algebraic Surfaces , A. Beauville
    2. Chapters on Algebraic Surfaces , M.Reid
    3. Compact Complex Surfaces, BHPV
    4. Works of G.Xiao, F.Catanese, O.Debarre,X.Lv-K.Zuo and etc
