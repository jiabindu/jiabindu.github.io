    Pre-requisite Courses

    Commutative Algebra, Algebraic Geometry

    Syllabus


    Chapter 1: Basics on algebraic surfaces


    1.1 Cubic surfaces. Note 1, Sep 9
    1.2 Curves on surfaces and intersection numbers. Note 2, Sep 16
    1.3 Blow-ups, rational maps and linear systems. Note 3, Sep 23
    1.4 Structure of birational maps. Note 4, Sep 28
    1.5 Exceptional curves, Du Val and rational singularities, singularities of double coverings. Note 5, Sep 30

    Chapter 2: Classification theory


    2.1 Nakai-Moishezon Criterion, Hodge index theorem and Kodaira dimension. Note 6, Oct 14
    2.2 Noether-Enriques theorem, geometrically ruled surfaces and rank 2 vector bundles on curves. Note 7, Oct 21
    2.3 Minimal models of ruled surfaces and Castelnuovo's rationality criterion. Note 8, Oct 28
    2.4 Elliptic surfaces and canonical bundle formula. Note 9, Nov 4
    2.5 K3 surfaces and Enriques surfaces
    2.6 Abelian surfaces and Albanese varieties
    2.7 Bi-elliptic surfaces
    2.8 Enriques-Kodaira classification via MMP

    Chapter 3: Fibrations of algebraic surfaces


    3.1 Slope inequality
    3.2 Hyperelliptic fibrations
    3.3 Canonical fibrations
    3.4 Albanese fibrations
    3.5 Fibrations of low genus

    Chapter 4: Geography of surfaces of general type


    4.1 Miyaoka-Yau inequality
    4.2 Noether, Debarre and Severi inequalities
    4.3 Geometry of pluri-canonical maps

    References:

    Chapters on Algebraic Surfaces, M. Reid
    Complex Algebraic Surfaces, A. Beauville
    Compact Complex Surfaces, BHPV
    Classification of Complex Algebraic Surfaces, C. Ciliberto
    The fibrations of algebraic surfaces, G. Xiao
    Surfaces fibrées en courbes de genre deux, G. Xiao
    Works of A. Beauville, F. Catanese, O. Debarre, E. Horikawa, G. Xiao, X. Lv-K. Zuo, M. Barja, R. Pardini, L. Stoppino and etc
