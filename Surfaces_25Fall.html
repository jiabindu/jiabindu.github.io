    Syllabus


    Chapter 1: Basics on algebraic surfaces


    1.1 Cubic surfaces. Note 1, Sep 9
    1.2 Curves on surfaces and intersection numbers. Note 2, Sep 16
    1.3 Blow-ups, rational maps and linear systems. Note 3, Sep 23
    1.4 Structure of birational maps. Note 4, Sep 28
    1.5 Exceptional curves, Du Val and rational singularities, singularities of double coverings. Note 5, Sep 30

    Chapter 2: Classification theory


    2.1 Nakai-Moishezon Criterion, Hodge index theorem and Kodaira dimension. Note 6, Oct 14
    2.2 Noether-Enriques theorem, geometrically ruled surfaces and rank 2 vector bundles on curves. Note 7, Oct 21
    2.3 Minimal models of ruled surfaces and Castelnuovo's rationality criterion. Note 8, Oct 28
    2.4 Morphisms to curves, Zariski lemma and elliptic fibrations. Note 9, Nov 4
    2.5 Canonical bundle formula, Kodaira's table of singular fibres and classification of elliptic surfaces. Note 10 , Nov 11
    2.6 K3 surfaces, curves on K3s, more examples. Note 11, Nov 18
    2.7 Enriques surfaces, curves and linear systems on Enriques surfaces. Note 12, Nov 25
    2.8 Abelian surfaces, Albanese morphisms, and surfaces with pg=0 and q≥1. Note 13, Dec 2
    2.9 Bi-elliptic surfaces. Note 14 , Dec 9

    Chapter 3: Geography of surfaces of general type


    3.1 Some general properties of surfaces of general type. Note 15 Dec 16
    3.2 Miyaoka-Yau inequality. Note 16, Dec 16
    3.3 Geometry of pluri-canonical maps. Note 17, Dec 16

    Appendix: Enriques-Kodaira classification via MMP.


    A.1 Surfaces with non-nef canonical bundle. Note 18, Dec 23
    A.2 Minimal Model Program for surfaces. Note 19 , Dec 23
    A.3 Surfaces with Kodaira dimension 0. Note 20, Dec 23

    Chapter 4*: Fibrations of algebraic surfaces


    4.1 Slope inequality
    4.2 Hyperelliptic fibrations
    4.3 Canonical fibrations
    4.4 Albanese fibrations
    4.5 Fibrations of low genus
