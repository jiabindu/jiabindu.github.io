    Email: jiabin.du@simis.cn

    Course information:

    Course time: every Tuesday 9:55-12:30 a.m., at SIMIS 1610

    Pre-requisite Courses

    Commutative Algebra, Algebraic Geometry

    Syllabus

    Week 1-5

    Goal: Basics on algebraic surfaces


    1. Cubic surfaces. Sep 9
    2. Curves on surfaces and intersection numbers. Sep 16
    3. Blow-ups, rational maps and linear systems. Sep 23
    4. Structure of birational morphisms and Castelnuovo contractibility theorem. Sep 28
    5. ADE, rational and cyclic singularities. Sep 30

    Week 6-9

    Goal: Classification theory


    1. Positivity of divisors
    2. Enriques-Kodaira classification on surfaces

    Week 10-12

    Goal: Fibrations of algebraic surfaces


    1. Slope inequality
    2. Hyperelliptic fibrations
    3. Canonical fibrations
    4. Albanese fibrations
    5. Fibrations of low genus

    Week 13-16

    Goal: Geography of surfaces of general type


    1. Miyaoka-Yau inequality
    2. Noether and Debarre inequalities
    3. Castelnuovo inequality
    4. Severi and Barja-Pardini-Stoppino inequalities
    5. Geometry of pluri-canonical maps
    6. Classification of surfaces with small invariants

    References:

    1. Chapters on Algebraic Surfaces, M. Reid
    2. Complex Algebraic Surfaces, A. Beauville
    3. Compact Complex Surfaces, BHPV
    4. Classification of Complex Algebraic Surfaces, C. Ciliberto
    5. The fibrations of algebraic surfaces, G. Xiao
    6. Surfaces fibrées en courbes de genre deux, G. Xiao
    7. Works of A. Beauville, F. Catanese, O. Debarre, E. Horikawa, G. Xiao, X. Lv-K. Zuo, M. Barja, R. Pardini, L. Stoppino and etc
