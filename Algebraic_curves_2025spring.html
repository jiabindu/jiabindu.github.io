    Syllabus

    Week 1-5

    Goal: Basics on algebraic varieties (介绍代数簇的基本概念):


    • Projective algebraic varieties(射影代数簇)
    • Morphisms and rational maps (态射和有理映射)
    • Irreducibility and dimension (不可约性和维数)
    • Algebraic curves and smooth varieties(代数曲线光滑簇)
    • Resolution of singularities (奇异曲线的奇点消解)
    • Intersection theory and Bezout Theorem (射影空间中的相交理论及Bezout Theorem )

    Week 6-10

    Goal: Riemann-Roch Theorem and applications(介绍复曲线的 Riemann-Roch 定理):


  • Divisors and vector bundles (除子和向量丛)
  • Cohomology (上同调)
  • Serre duality and Riemann-Roch Theorem
  • Riemann-Hurwitz formula
  • Special curves: rational,elliptic,hyperelliptic

    • Week 11-15

    Goal: Geometry of projective curves(介绍复射影曲线的几何)：


  • Embeddings into projective space and canonical maps (到射影空间的嵌入、典范映射)
  • Embeddings into abelian variety and Albanese morphisms (到阿贝尔簇的嵌入、Albanese态射)
  • Clifford inequality
  • Castelnuovo inequality
  • Brill-Noether theory (Brill-Noether理论)
