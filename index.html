About me

    Since July 2024, I am a postdoc at Shanghai Institute for Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences.

    I was a postdoc at SCMS, Fudan University in 2022-2024.

    I obtained my Ph.D degree under the supervision by Prof. Wenfei Liu at Xiamen University in June 2022.

    My research area is Algebraic Geometry, more precisely I am interested in geometry and topology of algebraic varieties of general type, including explicit geography problems, classification, automorphism groups, algebraic cycles and moduli problems.

    Moreover I am also interested in geometry of abelian varieties, e.g. algebraic cycles, Schottky problem, generic vanishing theory and their applications.

    I've recently been working on finding new tools to investigate explicit geography problems in algebraic surfaces and threefolds of general type.
