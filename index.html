About me

Since July 2022, I am a postdoc at SCMS, Fudan University.

I got my Ph.D degree in Xiamen University supervised by Prof. Wenfei Liu.

My research area is Algebraic Geometry, more precisely I am interested in questions/problems on algebraic cycles (e.g. Bloch-type conjecture, finite-dimensionality of Chow motives), algebraic surfaces (geography of irregular surfaces, classification of surfaces of general type with small invariants) and abelian varieties (generic vanishing).

From winter 2023, I am on the job market.
