About me

Since July 2024, I am a postdoc at Shanghai Institute for Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences.

I was a postdoc at SCMS, Fudan University in 2022-2024.

I obtained my Ph.D degree under the supervision by Prof. Wenfei Liu at Xiamen University in June 2022.

My research area is Algebraic Geometry, more precisely I am interested in geometry of algebraic surfaces, abelian varieties and irregular varieties (e.g. Bloch-type conjecture on surfaces, classification theory of surfaces with small invariants, geography of irregular varieties).
