Research Interests

    My research focuses on Algebraic Geometry. More precisely I am interested in the following topics:

    • Algebraic Surfaces
    • Geography of threefolds of general type
    • Abelian varieties
    • Algebraic cycles
    • Topology of algebraic varieties
